Philosophers

This section will feature major philosophers and their contributions.

  • Socrates

    A classical Greek philosopher who is considered one of the founders of Western philosophy. He is known for his method of questioning and dialogue, known as the Socratic method.

  • Plato

    A student of Socrates and teacher of Aristotle, Plato founded the Academy in Athens. He is known for his theory of forms and his work on political philosophy.

  • Aristotle

    A student of Plato and teacher of Alexander the Great, Aristotle contributed to many fields, including logic, ethics, and metaphysics. He is considered one of the greatest philosophers of all time.
