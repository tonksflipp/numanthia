Concepts

This section will explain key philosophical concepts and terms.

  • Ethics

    The branch of philosophy that deals with questions about what is morally right and wrong, good and bad, and justice.

  • Ontology

    The study of being, existence, and the categorization of entities in the world. It is concerned with what entities exist and how they can be categorized.

  • Epistemology

    The branch of philosophy concerned with the nature, scope, and limits of knowledge, including the study of belief, truth, and justification.

  • Metaphysics

    The branch of philosophy that explores the fundamental nature of reality, including questions about time, space, cause and effect, and the nature of objects and their properties.

  • Aesthetics

    The branch of philosophy that deals with the nature of beauty, art, and taste, and the creation and appreciation of beauty in the world.
