Ethics

Ethics is the branch of philosophy that deals with questions of morality, right and wrong, good and bad, virtue and vice, justice, and related concepts. It explores how individuals should act and the moral principles that guide human conduct in various situations. Ethical theories often discuss the foundations of moral judgments and how they can be applied in real-life scenarios.
