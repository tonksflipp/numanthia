Epistemology

Epistemology is the branch of philosophy concerned with the theory of knowledge. It explores the nature, scope, and limits of human knowledge. Epistemologists ask questions such as: What is knowledge? How do we acquire it? What are the sources and justification for our beliefs? Epistemology also investigates the distinction between belief, opinion, and knowledge.
