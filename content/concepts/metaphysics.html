Metaphysics

Metaphysics is a branch of philosophy that deals with the study of the fundamental nature of reality. It explores questions about existence, objects, time, space, causality, and what is possible or necessary in the world. It seeks to answer profound questions about what truly exists and how different aspects of reality are interconnected.
